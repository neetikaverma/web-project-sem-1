About Us

Get To Know Us

Happy Home was launched in September 2020.It is the largest home service provider in Canada with presence in India and Singapore.The platform helps customers book reliable home services like beauty services, massage therapy for men and women, cleaning, repair services, moving and packing, water services etc. at the comfort of home as our name suggests.The comapany's vision is to empower millions of service professionals across the world to deliver services at home like never seen before. The company partners with tens of thousands of service professionals, helping them training, credit, product, procurement, insurance, technology etc.

How we do it

Happy Home provides a platform that allows skilled and experienced professionals to connect with users looking for specific services. All the professionals, though experienced and skilled, undergo intensive training modules before being allowed to list their services on the platform. Once on platform, our match-making algorithm identifies professionals who are closest to the users' requirements and available at the requested time and date.
